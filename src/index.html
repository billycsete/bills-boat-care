Bill's Boat Care

Bill’s Boat Care specializes in maintenance and management of sail and power boats ranging from 30 to 60 feet in the Santa Barbara, CA area. Our goal is to always have the boat ready for the owner to take wherever the next adventure might be. We handle many tasks that owners don't care to do, have time for, or are too minor for vendors to accommodate. For larger jobs, the best of the local Santa Barbara vendors are utilized.

Some of the services we provide include:

  • Management of regular engine and generator maintenance
  • Outboard and dinghy maintenance
  • Fueling
  • Holding tank emptying
  • Work with local boat yards (Santa Barbara and Ventura) to ensure successful haul outs
  • Light repairs of fiberglass, electronics, plumbing, wood work and rigging
  • Working with local vendors on large projects for electronics, rigging, stainless, brightwork and cleaning
  • Regular checking of thru-hulls, bilge pumps, fluid levels, filters, strainers, etc
  • Pre and post trip boat checks
  • Available for delivery service on the US west coast
  • Available as crew for deliveries world wide

Referrals available upon request.
